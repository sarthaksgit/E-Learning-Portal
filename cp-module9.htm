AWS Auto Scaling

AWS Auto Scaling helps you maintain application availability and allows you to automatically scale your resources based on demand or a defined schedule. It optimizes the performance and costs of your applications by adjusting capacity to meet changing requirements.

AWS EC2 Auto Scaling

Amazon EC2 Auto Scaling is a specific feature within AWS Auto Scaling that automatically adjusts the number of Amazon EC2 instances in your application. You can define scaling policies based on various metrics, such as CPU utilization or network traffic. EC2 Auto Scaling ensures that the desired number of instances are available to handle the load and automatically replaces unhealthy instances.
