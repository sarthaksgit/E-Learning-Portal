Elastic Load Balancer (ELB)

Duration: 10 Hrs

Introduction

Explore the fundamentals of Elastic Load Balancer (ELB) and understand its role in distributing incoming traffic across multiple targets, enhancing the availability and fault tolerance of your applications.

How ELB Works

Dive into the mechanics of how Elastic Load Balancer works, including its load balancing algorithms and integration with Auto Scaling for dynamic resource allocation.

ELB Health Checks

Learn about health checks in ELB, which monitor the health of registered targets and automatically route traffic to healthy instances.

ELB Cross Zone Load Balancing

Explore Cross-Zone Load Balancing in ELB, which evenly distributes traffic across instances in all available Availability Zones.

ELB Positioning - Internet-facing vs Internal ELB

Understand the positioning of ELB as either Internet-facing or Internal, based on your application's architecture and security requirements.

Refresher for TCP IP Packet Flow

Get a refresher on the TCP/IP packet flow and how it relates to the load balancing process in Elastic Load Balancer.

ELB Health Check

Deep dive into ELB health checks, configuring parameters to determine the health status of instances and manage the flow of traffic.

ELB Security Groups

Learn how to configure security groups for Elastic Load Balancer to control inbound and outbound traffic, ensuring a secure and controlled network environment.
