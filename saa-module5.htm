Auto Scaling and Simple Notification Service (SNS)

Duration: 10 Hrs

Auto Scaling Components

Explore the key components of Auto Scaling, a service that automatically adjusts the number of EC2 instances in your applications based on demand or a defined schedule.

Auto Scaling Features

Learn about the features of Auto Scaling, including dynamic scaling, predictive scaling, and the ability to automatically replace unhealthy instances.

Auto Scaling Availability Zone Rebalance Feature

Understand the Availability Zone Rebalance feature in Auto Scaling, which optimizes the distribution of instances across Availability Zones.

Simple Notification Service (SNS)

Introduction to Simple Notification Service (SNS), a fully managed messaging service that enables the decoupling of distributed systems, microservices, and serverless applications.

SNS Notifications & Merging Auto Scaling Groups

Explore SNS notifications and how they can be utilized in merging Auto Scaling groups, enhancing the flexibility and management of your applications.
